How You Can Help

It costs $150 per day to care for one child at AGC. This cost includes housing, food, clothing, healthcare, counseling, and teaching parents. Every single investment you make in our children is critical to their success and well-being.

Change A Life

For as little as $50 a month, you can help guide a child to a brighter future. All proceeds go to supporting a receipient's future education, housing and living expenses.

What You Get

Boys and Girls Country will send you a monthly newsletter. Your receipent will send you annual updates on their success track and a quarterly thank you letter.

Mentor & Connect

As an alumni of Boys And Girls Country, your wisdom and guidance are invaluable. This tool will allow you to send letters of encouragements to your sponsored child.
